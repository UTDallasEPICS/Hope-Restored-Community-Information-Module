Domestic
Violence
Resources

There are resources to help victims and survivors of domestic violence. From emergency shelter to legal advocacy to food assistance for those transitioning to a life free of violence, there is help.

If you or a loved one is trapped in an abusive relationship, get help! Call our hotline at 833-903-SAFE (7233) for safety planning and referrals to area shelters and victim services resources.

Our hotline is open Mon-Fri 8:00am - 5:00pm CT
